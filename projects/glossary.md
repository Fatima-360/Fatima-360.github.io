# Fintech Technical Glossary
**Target Audience:** Engineering and Finance Teams

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Audit Trail** | A chronological, tamper-proof record of system activities and financial transactions. |
| **Disbursal** | The physical act of paying out money from a fund or bank account. |
| **Reconciliation** | The process of ensuring two sets of records (e.g., bank statement vs. app data) are in agreement. |
| **VAT Compliance** | Ensuring that Value Added Tax is correctly calculated and reported per EU Directive 2006/112/EC. |
| **SaaS** | Software as a Service; a software licensing and delivery model in which software is licensed on a subscription basis. |
