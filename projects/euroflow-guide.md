# EuroFlow Quick-Start Guide: Submitting Expenses
**Target Audience:** Remote Employees

## Overview
This guide helps you submit business expenses for reimbursement using the EuroFlow mobile app.

## Steps to Submit
1. **Capture Receipt:** Take a clear photo of your VAT receipt.
2. **Assign Category:** Select from 'Travel', 'Meals', or 'Office Supplies'.
3. **Check VAT:** Ensure the VAT amount matches the receipt total.
4. **Submit:** Hit the "Send for Approval" button.

> **Note:** Reimbursements are processed every Friday at 14:00 CET.
