# Fintech Technical Glossary
**Purpose:** Standardizing terminology for the EuroFlow Engineering and Finance teams.

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Audit Trail** | A chronological record of system activities. |
| **Disbursal** | The act of paying out money from a fund. |
| **Reconciliation** | Ensuring two sets of records (bank vs. app) match. |
| **VAT** | Value Added Tax required for EU tax compliance. |
