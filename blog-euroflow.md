# 7 Ways to Speed Up Your Monthly Expense Reporting

Managing expenses shouldn't be a full-time job. Whether you're a freelancer or part of a global team, efficiency is key to getting reimbursed faster.

### 1. Snap Photos Immediately
Don't wait until the end of the month. Use the **EuroFlow App** to snap a photo of your receipt the moment you get it. This ensures you never lose a VAT-compliant receipt.

### 2. Monitor Your Status
Understanding the difference between **Pending** and **Approved** helps you predict when money will hit your account. Our latest guide explains the "Flagged" status so you can fix errors before they cause delays.
