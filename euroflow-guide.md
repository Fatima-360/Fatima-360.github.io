# EuroFlow Quick-Start Guide: Submitting Expenses

This guide helps remote employees submit business expenses for reimbursement using the EuroFlow mobile app.

## Steps to Submit
1. Open the **EuroFlow App**.
2. Navigate to **Expenses** > **New Entry**.
3. Upload a photo of your receipt.
4. Click **Submit for Approval**.
